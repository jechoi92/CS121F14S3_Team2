Asteroids will come down from the top of the screen. Your job is to prevent them from crashing into the Earth. Each one will have fractions on it. You can destroy the asteroid by solving the expression. If the asteroid just has one fraction, simplify it.

Move the ship by dragging it side to side to line it up with the asteroid you want to destroy.

Fire a laser by clicking on one of the buttons on the right hand side. If the laser frequency solves the expression on the asteroid, the asteroid will explode.
